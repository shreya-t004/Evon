Uttarakhand Open Seniors Tennis Tournament 2023

9th -10th December 2023

Factsheet and rules:

1) Categories : A (75+combined), B (90+ combined), C (105+ combined), D (120+ combined), Lucky Doubles. This is Only Doubles Tournament.

2) Lucky Doubles Format - Any participant who loses both the matches in the first round shall be considered for the draw of Lucky Doubles. Pairing up logic will be decided later(Work in Progress).

3) Age Limit is 35 years.

4) The age of any participant shall be calculated as his running age as on 9th December. For example if the participant turns 34 on the 8th December, he can be considered as 35.

5) One player can participate in max 2 categories (excluding lucky doubles which could be the 3rd for any participant).

6) Coaches are allowed to play in Category A only. Any Individual who earns via tennis coaching shall be defined as Coach.

7) Entry Fee for two events is 4500 (6000 with double sharing accommodation of up to 2 days) and one event is 3000 (4500 with double sharing accommodation of up to 2 days).

8) Lunch shall be provided on both days and Gala dinner on the 9th December.

9) Every participant shall get Indian Tree T-Shirt, Shorts, Socks, Cap, Wristband

10) Prize money for the winner team is 21000 and for the runners up team is 11000. Each Semi-Finalist team shall get 4000. Total Prize money is Rs. 2 lacs.

11) Last date for entry Fees is 6th December.

12) No entry fees shall at all be accepted after 6th December.

13) Draws and Order of Play shall be published on the 7th December so that out-stationed players can plan accordingly.

14) If any team does not turn up at scheduled time, walk-over shall be given to the opponent within 15 minutes.

15) Balls for the Tournament shall be Head Tour.

16) For any query Please contact Tournament Director Sumit Goel (Ph. 9412977857)

18) Location of Om farms where stay is arranged
